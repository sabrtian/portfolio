About

A series of digital illustrations inspired by family, friends, travels, everyday experiences, and my favorite foods.

At the beginning of 2020, I made the goal of becoming a better observer and visual storyteller. As small doodles started to reflect more of my personal experiences and beliefs, I came to the realization of how I view and experience food. After many late night doodles, I believe food is much more than something we eat - it tells us about our experiences, not only when we eat it but also in its origins and the culture. Below is a selection of food illustrations made with Procreate and inspired by family, friends, travels, everyday experiences, and of course, my favorite foods.
