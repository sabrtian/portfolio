A collection of self-designed and developed websites using Figma, HTML, and CSS.

01. 20/20 VISION

20/20 Vision is a project reflecting on the reactions to COVID-19 pandemic. For some, 2021 is a new start, a time to return to "normal life" in China, but for others, such as those in the U.S., it's a continuation of the previous' years efforts in fighting the pandemic. This project was made to highlight Asian American experiences during the pandemic and the reactions to events throughout the year. It was an attempt to highlight the similarities in the everyday experience of just surviving.

02. MAINTENANCE WORKERS

These locations attract some of the largest crowds globally, and are used for a variety of reasons: necessity, tourism, religious, social, and activism. Their visitors also tend to leave things behind, purposefully or not, yet these spaces are always clean for the next day. This project aims to highlight maintenance workers, some permanent and some temporary. Their efforts and hard work allows for the continual streams of visitors and the longevity of that space.

03. CLOCK

An exploration of digitally visualizing time where the speed of each circle represents (from top to bottom) hour, minute, second, and millisecond.
