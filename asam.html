About

An ongoing research project on contemporary data art created within the 20th century to visualize global migration patterns from Asia to the U.S.

I am currently researching contemporary data-focused art created within the 20th century to visualize global migration patterns from Asia to the U.S. and shift the power dynamic in defining American history and art to tell the narratives of AAPI communities from everyday individuals within. I also designed this year's fellowship logo, which consists of open quotation marks and an ellipsis to symbolize the honest conversations we are initiating through our research projects.
