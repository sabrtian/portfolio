About

Nouri is a food subscription startup focused on creating healthy meals for pregnant mothers, customized to each mother’s trimester.

Nouri believes in the power of food’s impact on us physically and emotionally, are invested in the wellness of their community, and are inspired by Eastern food therapy to customize meals to each mother’s trimester.

I worked with another designer to develop the logo and sticker. Since the company’s primary focus is on resilience, hope, and new beginnings for mothers, we incorporated gingko imagery to symbolize this. We also surrounded the ginkgo with a circle to represent the bowls of nourishment that Nouri provides to its community.
