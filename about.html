02. WHY DO I DESIGN

To me, design is a form of advocacy. Everything we create and use shape our behaviors and surrounding environments. As designers, we must advocate for all users in creating responsible, accessible, and impactful ways to engage with both analog and digital spaces around us. I'm fascinated in analyzing the standards of our society, finding ways to refine them to be more inclusive, and designing for them sustainably.
