Sabrina Tian is a Chinese-American designer and researcher interested in designing for culture , whether it be in collaboration with microorganisms, the social behavior and norms found in human societies, or the system of interactions between a society with the environment.

To her, design is a form of advocacy. Everything we create and use shape our behaviors and surrounding environments. As designers, we must advocate for all species in creating responsible, accessible, and impactful ways to engage with both analog and digital spaces around us. She is fascinated in analyzing the standards of our society, finding ways to refine them to be more inclusive, and designing for them sustainably.
