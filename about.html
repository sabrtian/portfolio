About

01. who am i

Designer and researcher studying at Design and Cognitive Science with a Computer Science minor at the University of Pennsylvania who believes in the power of design to make an impact, and cares about how technology can shape human behavior and experiences.

02. why do i design

Everything we create and use shape our behaviors and surrounding environments. As designers, we must advocate for all users in creating responsible, accessible, and impactful ways to engage with both analog and digital spaces around us. I'm fascinated in analyzing the standards of our society, finding ways to refine them to be more inclusive, and designing for them sustainably.

03. after hours

You can also find me listening to science podcasts, watching mini documentaries, and making digital drawings.
